Our Story

COMMA was born from our founders' frustration with traditional office spaces and the isolation of remote work. We set out to create something different – spaces that inspire productivity while fostering genuine professional connections.

Since opening our first location in 2020, we've grown to serve over 500 professionals across three premium locations, maintaining our commitment to exceptional design, community, and service.
